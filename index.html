The Provider's Bill of Rights

  • Clinical Freedom

    Make patient care decisions free from external pressures.

  • Timely Authorizations

    Get approvals for necessary treatments without delays.

  • Respect for Expertise

    Ensure your skills and training are valued above cost-saving algorithms.

  • Fair Compensation

    Receive payment for your care without unjust delays.

  • Data Ownership

    Maintain control of your data, ensuring it serves you and your patients—not payor profits.

  • CAdvocacy Without Reprisal

    Speak out for change without fear of retaliation.
